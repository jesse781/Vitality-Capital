The Opportunity
Doubling Capital Deployment for a Green New York

NY Green Bank has deployed $2.5B+ into clean energy—but billions more sit waiting for the right partners. The challenge? A market filled with subscale operators too small to absorb institutional capital efficiently.

The gap: NYGB's ratepayer-funded mandate demands proven, cash-generating platforms. The reality: fragmented climate businesses that can't meet their deployment thresholds.

The opportunity: Korea Development Bank will match any investment from NY Green Bank dollar-for-dollar with a 7% first-loss tranche—immediately doubling capital deployment up to $150M for a Green New York.

Vitality Capital bridges this gap by acquiring, consolidating, and professionalizing fragmented operators—transforming subscale businesses into institutional-grade platforms purpose-built for NYGB's capital deployment.

Exit Strategy & Risk Mitigation

Accelerated Exit Timeline

Our portfolio discipline and private equity approach drives companies to consolidate, merge, and explore acquisitions quickly. Target: 2-year max hold for each company—driving high alpha returns for investors through rapid value creation and strategic exits.

First-Loss Protection

Korea Development Bank provides a 7% first-loss tranche on all matched capital—protecting NYGB's ratepayer-funded investment with institutional-grade downside protection while maintaining full upside participation.
